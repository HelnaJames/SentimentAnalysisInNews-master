Sweden's PLM lifts H1 profits.:Positive
Swedish beverage can maker PLM AB on Tuesday posted an increase in profits for the first six months of 1996, saying the improvement would be maintained for the rest of the year.:Negative
PLM, which was split out of investment group Industrivarden in November last year, reported an increase of just 10 million crowns ($1.5 million) in earnings after financial items to 229 million crowns.:Very negative
However, the bottle, can and plastics maker said  profits for the second quarter in isolation had risen 14 percent to 167 million crowns.:Neutral
This improvement would last for the rest of the year, the company said.:Negative
"Despite weaker market development in the early part of the summer, our existing forecast of a higher result after financial items for 1996 is unchanged," PLM said in its half-year report.:Negative
