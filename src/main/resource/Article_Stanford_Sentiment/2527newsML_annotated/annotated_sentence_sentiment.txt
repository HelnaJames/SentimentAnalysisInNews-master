AMEX to extend use of electronic book.:Negative
The American Stock Exchange (AMEX) said on Tuesday it signed an agreement with OM Stockholm to extend the use of the AMEX Options Display Book (AODB) to its options specialists floorwide.:Negative
AODB is a fully-automated electronic book designed to replace paper books currently used by options specialists.:Negative
The agreement followed a five-month pilot phase during which more than 20 percent of AMEX equity options were transferred to AODB, the exchange said in a statement.:Negative
The AMEX said it expected all current and future options and options-related products to be on the electronic book within 12 months.:Negative
