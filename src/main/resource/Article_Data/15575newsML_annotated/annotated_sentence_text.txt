Belgrade newspapers are full of speculation that Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic has promised that advisers from NatWest Markets of Britain would help launch the privatisation of some parts of the company.
A spokesman for NatWest Markets in London declined to confirm this
All he said was that his company was an adviser to the government and that the general subject of privatisations was being examined.
"Natwest Markets, as advisers to the government, are looking into potential privatisations," the spokesman said without detailing which possible privatisations were being studied.
