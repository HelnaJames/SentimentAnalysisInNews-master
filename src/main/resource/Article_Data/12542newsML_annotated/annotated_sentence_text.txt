Polish venture starts to assemble Hyundai cars.
A joint-venture of Polish trader PHZ Universal SA and South Korea's Hyundai has started to assemble Hyundai cars, Zycie Warszawy daily said on Saturday
It said the company started to assemble the Accent family car model on Friday from kits supplied by Hyundai and that it would make some 1,500 cars this year.
It said the price of the Polish-made Accent would remain unchanged at 34,000 to 46,0000 zlotys
Earlier this month Poland's Industry Ministry gave Universal the go-ahead to begin production of 15,000 Hyundai cars annually
