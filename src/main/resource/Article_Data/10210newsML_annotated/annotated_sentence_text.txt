Mexican shares tumble on profit-taking.
Mexican stocks ran out of steam on Friday after a week of record highs gave way to profit-taking, although stock prices made up some ground toward the close of trade after falling through support levels.
The IPC index of leading stocks fell 24.96 points or 0.74 percent to 3,364.37.
The market was very weak
Most of the people paid to see what was going on and got out early," one floor trader said.

Traders said the fact that stocks bottomed out at the second resistance level of 3,332 points and rebounded was a good sign that stocks are consolidating in preparation to rise again.
