The Beijing Municipal Higher People's Court sentenced Hong Yang, 44, a Chinese national, to five years in prison for accepting bribes totalling 100,000 yuan ($12,000) in 1993, the Xinhua news agency said.
"The sentence was dealt with leniently ... and according to law," Xinhua said, quoting the court.
Hong, a former employee of the People's Bank of China, or central bank, was arrested in December while on an IMF mission to China and was found guilty on June 28.
 He was then sentenced to 10 years in jail, but appealed.
