 Ohio Blue Cross approves $299.5 mln Columbia/HCA deal
Columbia/HCA Healthcare Corp.'s proposed $299.5 million acquisition of most of Blue Cross &amp; Blue Shield of Ohio cleared a hurdle Tuesday when the health insurer's policyholders approved the deal at a special meeting, Blue Cross said
About 60 protestors outside the meeting outnumbered the 38 who attended it
Most proxies were mailed in advance of the meeting, a Blue Cross spokesman said
Protestors, carrying signs reading, "Stop the Merger," said they represented retirees, trade union members and others. Blue Cross is currently a not-for-profit entity.
