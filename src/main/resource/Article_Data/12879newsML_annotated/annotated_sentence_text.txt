R.E.M. signs recording contract with Warner Bros.
Warner Bros. Records said on Sunday it signed a contract with rock band R.E.M., reportedly worth $80 million, the largest recording contract in history.
"Yes, we have signed a new contract with R.E.M.," Warner Bros. Records Chairman Russ Thyret told Reuters.
