BASKETBALL-GINEBRA MAUL SHELL IN PBA
MANILA 1996-08-24Ginebra San Miguel routed Formula Shell 120-103 on Friday evening at the close of the semifinal round of the Philippine Basketball Association second conference
The two teams will meet again in a playoff on Sunday to decide which of them will face early finalist Alaska Milk in a best-of-seven series for the title
The Gins set the tone for the match early when they raced to a 23-point lead in the first quarter against aShell teamweakenedby the absence of injured team captain Benjie Paras and point guard Ronnie Magsanoc
In the second game, AlaskabeatPurefoods Hotdogs103-95 in overtime
