GOLF-THUNDERSTORMSFORCE SUSPENSIONOFSECOND ROUND IN AKRON
AKRON, Ohio 1996-08-23Thunderstorms forced thesuspensionof theWorld Series of Golfafter just five players in the 43-man field had completed the second round on Friday
Playinitially wasinterruptedfor more than 3-1/2 hours before resuming for two hours
Butplaywas finallysuspendedfor the day when the storm continued
The 38 players are schedeled to resume their rounds on Saturday morning before the third round begins
