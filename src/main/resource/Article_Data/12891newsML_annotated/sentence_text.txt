UK: Bass/Allied agree new beer terms
Bass/Allied agree new beer terms
LONDON 1996-08-25Allied Domecq Plcsaid on Sunday that it has agreed new beer supply terms with Bass Plc
Alliedsaid Bass will, if required, supply substantially more than half of Allied Domecq Retailing's requirements, including Tetley Bitter, in 1998 and 1999, at pre-agreed levels of discount
Existing beer supply agreements between Carlsberg-Tetley and Allied Domecq Retailing will continue on their current basis until December 12 1997,Alliedsaid
After that, ADR will be free to buy its beer from any supplier at commercial rates
Alliedsaid 63 million stg of the proceeds from the sale of its stake in Carlsberg-Tetley to Bass will be used in thebalance sheet as a provision for uneconomic beer supply
This will be released to the profit and loss account during the supply agreement until December 12 next year
