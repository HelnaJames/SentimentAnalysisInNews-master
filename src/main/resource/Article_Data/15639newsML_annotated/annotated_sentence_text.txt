The Crossgate acquisition would be earnings enhancing from day one and provided a good platform for Regent Inns to expand its geographical presence on a regional basis, the company said.
Regent Inns buys Crossgate Leisure, expands.
Regent Inns Plc said on Tuesday it was buying Crossgate Leisure Ltd, which operates 18 estates of cafe bars, snooker and sports bars in north and south England, for 6.28 million stg.
The group, which owns 46 public houses, hotels and restaurants, said it also planned a geographic expansion of its pub estate through a new programme of site acquisitions in the midlands and north of England.
