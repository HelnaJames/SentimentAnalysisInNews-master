TENNIS-FERREIRA ADVANCES TO CANADIAN OPEN SEMIFINALS.
Third-seeded Wayne Ferreira of South Africa won a 7-5 6-2 baseline battle with third-seeded Thomas Enqvist of Sweden in the quarterfinals of the $2 million Canadian Open on Friday.
Ferreira, ranked 10th in the world, plays seventh-seeded Todd Martin of the United States in one of Saturday's semifinals.
