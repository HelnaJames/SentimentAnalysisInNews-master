KANSAS CITY, Mo

, citing problems facing the online service industry, said Wednesday it has decided not to complete the spin-off of the rest of its Compuserve Corp
 subsidiary, at least for now

-based company, which owns 80 percent of Compuserve after spinning off part of the online service earlier this year, citedCompuserve'srecent and projected losses and uncertainties facing the online industry
