Respironics Inc. said Monday it signed a definitive agreement to buy Colorado-based Lifecare International Inc. for $50 million.
Both companies make respiratory therapy products.
For the fiscal year ended March 31, 1996, Lifecare had revenues of $33.1 million, Respironics said.
Respironics said it will finance the acquisition principally from proceeds of its recent stock offering.
The deal is expected to be completed in October, the company said
