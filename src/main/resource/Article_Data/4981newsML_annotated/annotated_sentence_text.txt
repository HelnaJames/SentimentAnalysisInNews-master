Campbell to cut staff, sell Swanson -- report.
Campbell Soup Co. is planning to cut its headquarters staff of 1,500, shut one of four major soup plants and divest some brand names, including Swanson frozen foods, the Philadelphia Inquirer reported Wednesday
A Campbell spokesman called the newspaper report speculative but did not deny it, which cited company sources and Wall Street analysts.
"We are engaged in a process to unlock resources to grow our company," said Campbell spokesman Jerry Buckley.
He acknowledged that Campbell has commissioned a study of operations by the management consulting firm Coopers &amp; Lybrand.
"We use consultants all the time," he said.
