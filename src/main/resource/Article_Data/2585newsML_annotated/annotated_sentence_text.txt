Greek PM Simitis to call early elections on Friday.

Greek Prime Minister Costas Simitis looks set to call early elections on Friday citing as reasons the economy and relations with Turkey, government officials said on Tuesday.
We expect the announcement on Friday after a special cabinet meeting
 All indications at the moment show that the premier will call early elections to take place in late September or early October," one official told Reuters.
Government spokesman Dimitris Reppas reiterated on Tuesday that Simitis will announce his final decision on the matter this week.
 Elections are now scheduled for October next year.
